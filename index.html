Found something?

Call the clerk and say what you've got.

Say what it is, where you found it, and roughly when. Hold on to it — the clerk will add it to the board and call you back as soon as there's a match.

Lost something?

Call the clerk and say what's missing.

Say what it is, where you last had it, and when. If someone's already handed it in, the clerk will know. If not, it goes on the board and you'll hear as soon as something comes in.
